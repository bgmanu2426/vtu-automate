A course can take up to 20 mins to complete. Until then, sit and relax.

Before you launch

  • Use your online.vtu.ac.in login, not your university portal credentials.
  • The course slug is extracted from full URL automatically.
  • You will get a Job ID - save it to reconnect if you close the tab.
  • Your password is never stored and wiped from memory on completion.

Queue system

  • Jobs run in order. During high traffic you will see your queue position.
  • Close the tab anytime - your job keeps running. Come back with Job ID to check.
